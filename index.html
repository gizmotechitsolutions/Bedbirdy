About Bedbirdy

At Bedbirdy, we believe that true comfort comes with responsibility. Founded in Mumbai, India, we are on a mission to transform your sleep sanctuary by offering bedding that feels as good for your soul as it does for your skin.

We merge luxurious comfort with a deep respect for our planet, proving you don't have to choose between a good night's sleep and a good conscience.

Our Sustainability Commitment

Sustainability isn't just a buzzword at Bedbirdy; it's woven into every thread. We are committed to a process that ensures minimal environmental impact while delivering maximum comfort to your home.

Responsible Materials

We use 100% natural fibers, including sustainably sourced cotton and eco-friendly, low-impact dyes.

Conscious Production

Our manufacturing processes reduce water consumption, energy use, and textile waste.

Ethical Practices

We partner with suppliers who uphold fair trade principles and provide safe working conditions.
